- **Impact 4:** 6+ distinct requesters across the invoice sub-cluster. Replaces daily manual work. Clear, urgent demand.
- **Feasibility 4:** ID-010 is trivially simple. ID-009 and ID-011 are moderate. ID-012 and ID-023 are straightforward.
